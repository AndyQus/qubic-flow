# QubicFlow — Discord Post (English)

**QubicFlow — Self-Hosted Qubic Wallet Tracker**

Hey everyone! I've been working on a tool over the past few weeks that I'd love to share with the community.

**What is QubicFlow?**
A self-hosted wallet tracker built specifically for Qubic — your data stays on your own machine/server, nothing goes to third parties.

**Features:**
- Track unlimited wallets (private & business)
- Automatic sync every 60 seconds via RPC or BOB Node
- Real-time WebSocket updates directly in the UI
- Daily EUR/USD price history from CoinGecko
- German & English, dark/light mode

**Tax Tools:**
- FIFO / LIFO / HIFO / AVCO calculation methods
- Country-specific rules (DE, AT, CH and more) incl. 1-year tax exemption
- CSV export for CoinTracking & your accountant
- PDF tax report at the click of a button

**Start with a single command:**
```
docker compose up --build
```
Also available as an Umbrel app (Community App Store).
